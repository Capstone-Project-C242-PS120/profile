Our team aims to create SiTeja, an application that deciphers nutritional information from packaged food and beverages. The project addresses the issue of unclear and inaccurate nutritional information on packaged products in Indonesia. SiTeja uses scanner technology and machine learning to provide NutriScore information, empowering Indonesians to make healthier food choices. We adopt a **design thinking approach** to solve this problem effectively, focusing on raising awareness and understanding of balanced nutrition.
